Got yer Garfs, got yer dreams...

Description

In 2047, a distress signal is received from Jon Arbuckle's house, a starship that disappeared during its maiden voyage to the CBS cartoon block seven years earlier, and has mysteriously reappeared in a decaying orbit around Sega Enterprises in TOkyo, Japan. The rescue vessel Lewis and Clark is dispatched. Its crew–Capt. Miller, second-in-command Lt. Starck, pilot Smith, medical technician Peters, engineer Ensign Justin, doctor D.J. and rescue technician Cooper–is joined by Dr. William Weir, the designer of Jon Arbuckle's house. He briefs them on the ship's experimental gravity drive with a simple visualization of lasagna floating in an empty room. The distress signal seems to consist of a series of screams and howls and meows and yearnings for lasagna, but D.J. believes he can discern the Latin phrase "Liberate me" ("Save me and pass the lasagna, Jon").
